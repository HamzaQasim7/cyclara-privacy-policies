Data Subject Rights

Effective Date: November 14, 2025

Important: Since Cyclara stores all data locally on your device with no server transmission, you have direct and complete control over your data. Most data rights can be exercised directly within the app without contacting us.

Your Rights Under GDPR and International Privacy Laws

Cyclara respects your data protection rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other applicable privacy laws. This document explains your rights and how to exercise them.

1. Right to Access (GDPR Art. 15)

What It Means:

You have the right to access all personal data stored about you.

How to Exercise:

  1. Open Cyclara app
  2. Navigate to Settings
  3. View all your tracked data in the app interface
  4. All data is immediately accessible at all times

Response Time: Immediate (data is always visible in the app)

2. Right to Data Portability (GDPR Art. 20)

What It Means:

You have the right to receive your data in a machine-readable format and transfer it to another service.

How to Exercise:

  1. Open Cyclara app
  2. Go to Settings ‚Üí Data Management
  3. Tap Export Data
  4. Choose export format (JSON/CSV)
  5. Save or share the exported file

Response Time: Immediate (self-service feature)

3. Right to Erasure / "Right to be Forgotten" (GDPR Art. 17)

What It Means:

You have the right to delete all your personal data.

How to Exercise:

Option 1 - Delete Specific Data:

  1. Open Cyclara app
  2. Navigate to the data you wish to delete (periods, symptoms, etc.)
  3. Swipe or tap delete on individual entries

Option 2 - Delete All Data:

  1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Data Management
  2. Tap Delete All Data
  3. Confirm deletion

Option 3 - Complete Removal:

  1. Uninstall the Cyclara app from your device
  2. All data is permanently removed from your device

Response Time: Immediate (data is deleted instantly)

4. Right to Rectification (GDPR Art. 16)

What It Means:

You have the right to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.

How to Exercise:

  1. Open Cyclara app
  2. Navigate to the data entry you wish to modify
  3. Tap Edit
  4. Update the information
  5. Save changes

Response Time: Immediate (self-service editing)

5. Right to Restriction of Processing (GDPR Art. 18)

What It Means:

You have the right to limit how your data is processed.

How to Exercise:

  1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Notifications
  2. Disable notifications to stop reminder processing
  3. Optionally, stop using specific features (symptoms tracking, etc.)

Note: Since data is only processed locally for your benefit, you can simply stop using features you don't want processed.

6. Right to Object (GDPR Art. 21)

What It Means:

You have the right to object to data processing.

How to Exercise:

Since Cyclara does not share data with third parties or use it for marketing, you can object to any processing by:

  • Disabling specific app features in Settings
  • Deleting specific data categories
  • Uninstalling the app

7. Right to Withdraw Consent (GDPR Art. 7.3)

What It Means:

You can withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

How to Exercise:

  1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Permissions
  2. Revoke notification or storage permissions
  3. Or delete the app entirely to withdraw all consent

8. Right to Lodge a Complaint

What It Means:

You have the right to file a complaint with a supervisory authority if you believe your data protection rights have been violated.

EU Users:

Contact your national data protection authority. Find your authority at: edpb.europa.eu

UK Users:

Information Commissioner's Office (ICO): ico.org.uk

US Users (California):

California Attorney General's Office: oag.ca.gov/privacy

9. Automated Decision-Making and Profiling

Cyclara uses algorithms to predict your menstrual cycle based on your historical data. This is done entirely on your device for your benefit. You have the right to:

  • Understand how predictions are made (based on cycle averages and patterns)
  • Manually override any predictions by editing your data
  • Disable predictive features in Settings

10. Data Breach Notification

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to external servers, the risk of a data breach from our side is eliminated. Your data security depends on:

  • Your device's security (PIN, biometrics, encryption)
  • Physical security of your device
  • Your device's operating system security updates

11. Contact for Data Rights Requests

While most rights can be exercised directly in the app, you may contact us for:

  • Questions about data rights
  • Technical assistance with exercising your rights
  • Filing formal complaints
