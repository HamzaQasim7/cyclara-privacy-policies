Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 14, 2025

Summary: Cyclara stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on external servers. Your period tracking data remains completely private and under your control.

1. Information We Collect

Cyclara collects and stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Period Data: Start dates, end dates, cycle length, and flow intensity
  • Symptom Tracking: Physical and emotional symptoms you choose to record
  • Reminders: Notification preferences and reminder settings
  • App Settings: Theme preferences and personalization options

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively on your device to:

  • Calculate and predict your menstrual cycle
  • Display personalized insights and trends
  • Send local notifications and reminders
  • Provide educational content relevant to your cycle phase

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using encrypted databases. We do not:

  • Upload your data to cloud servers
  • Share your information with third parties
  • Require account creation or login
  • Track your activity across other apps or websites

4. Permissions

Cyclara requests the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To send period and symptom tracking reminders
  • Storage: To save and backup your data locally on your device

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and not for data collection.

5. Third-Party Services

Cyclara does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app functions completely offline after installation.

6. Data Security

Your data is protected through:

  • Local encryption on your device
  • No internet transmission of personal data
  • Device-level security controls

7. Data Retention and Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
  • You can export your data at any time through the app's settings
  • Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data

8. Children's Privacy

Cyclara is designed for individuals who menstruate. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Parents should supervise their children's use of the app.

9. International Users

Since all data is stored locally on your device, no cross-border data transfer occurs. This policy complies with GDPR, CCPA, and other international privacy regulations.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified through the app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Your Rights

Under data protection laws (including GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access your data (available anytime in the app)
  • Export your data (available in Settings)
  • Delete your data (available in Settings or by uninstalling)
  • Opt-out of notifications (available in Settings)

For detailed information about exercising these rights, see our Data Subject Rights page.
